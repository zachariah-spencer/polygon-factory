Upgrading Objects:
	-Hovering the mouse over an object should bring up a tooltip with upgrade options.
	-If player has enough polygons they can purchase an upgrade which will adjust the multiplier and the speed of a generator.

Booster:
	- A ring with an orb that floats in orbit around it that can be piloted by the player
	- When piloted, the player can try to press the space bar when the orb is over a highlighted portion of the circle
	- Each successfully timed press will speed up the rotation of the orb, increasing the difficulty
	- Holding certain scorestreaks without messing this up will boost the production of all polygons throughout the factory
	- The moment the player exits the cockpit or fails to time a boost, the polygon production will return to normal
